KPDC Showcases

About

The showcase is KPDC's end-of-semester event where our members perform various dances in the form of subunits and projects. Guests organizations are also invited to perform and set up food booths to sell during the event.

The Fall KPDC Showcase will be held in the CUC Rangos Ballroom at 8:00pm on December 1, 2017.

Promo material and the Sign Up form will be released Fall 2017.

Contact

You can reach our Showcase Coordinator, Qian Weng, at qweng@andrew.cmu.edu.
