Fall Kick-Off!

Thursday, September 7th 5:00-6:00pm @ UC Activities Room

We welcome everyone back to kick-off the new semester! We will give a short presentation introducing the club structure and talk about changes for this year, followed by our first open class. New and old members are encouraged to attend to get a chance to meet new people!

If you can't wait to meet us, come swing by our booth at the Fall Activities Fair, Wednesday September 6 from 4:30-6:30pm on CFA Lawn.

KPDC Social

Friday, November 17th 7:00pm @ CUC Conan

The social is KPDC's midsemester get-together for all club members!

Promotional material and the Facebook Event will be released Fall 2017.

You can reach our Vice President, Jin Fang, at jfang1@andrew.cmu.edu.

KPDC Showcase

Friday, December 1st 8:00pm @ Rangos Ballroom

The showcase is KPDC's end-of-semester event where our members perform various dances in the form of subunits and projects. Guests organizations are also invited to perform and set up food booths to sell during the event. Promotional material and the Sign Up form will be released Fall 2017.

You can reach our Showcase Coordinator, Qian Weng, at qweng@andrew.cmu.edu.
