Who we are

Carnegie Mellon's K-Pop Dance Club was founded in 2015 by a bunch of freshmen who loved K-Pop and wanted to share it on campus.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide opportunities for K-Pop fans and aspiring dancers alike to grow as performers in an enthusiastic environment.
