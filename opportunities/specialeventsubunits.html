Special Event Subunits

About

Special event subunits are dances for specific events on or off campus, such as CMU's Dancers' Symposium or Culture Night. Auditions will be held throughout the year depending on the event.

Schedule

The Special Events Subunit schedule will be released in Fall 2017.

Audition Process

Attend the audition for a special event subunit to learn a portion of the choreo. The subunit leader will then record everyone's progress at the end to determine audition results.

Contact

You can reach one of our Dance Directors, Justin Yook, at jyook@andrew.cmu.edu.
