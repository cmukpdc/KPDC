Cover Contests

About

Cover contest dances are formed for 1theK Dance Cover Contests. These contests occur a few times within the semester and end within around a week and a half. Cover contest members will have to practice almost every day in order to film and edit a video before the deadline. Cover contests are a great opportunity to be exposed to intensive dancing! You can see our YouTube playlist here.

Audition Process

Some cover contests will be performed by KPDCrew. Other contests will be led by a dance instructor or will be left up for grabs to the general club members.

If it is of the latter, attend the audition (information will be released through Facebook group or email) for a cover contest dance to learn a portion of the choreo. The leader will then record everyone's progress at the end to determine audition results.
