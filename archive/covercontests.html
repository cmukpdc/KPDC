Cover Contests

About

Cover contest dances are formed for 1theK Dance Cover Contests. These contests occur a few times within the semester and end within around a week and a half. Cover contest members will have to practice almost every day in order to film and edit a video before the deadline. Cover contests are a great opportunity to be exposed to intensive dancing! Process

When a cover contest is released, dibs go to...

  1. 1. Existing projects of the same song

  2. 2. KPDCrew

  3. 3. Dance instructors

  4. 4. Open to the club

Announcements for auditions will be posted in the Facebook group or through email.
