ABOUT

NEW THIS YEAR! Introducing: KPDC Big & Little pairs! This is meant to be an opportunity for the newer and older members of our club to connect, and a chance for everyone to make new friends! Don't be intimidated about commitment - this is meant to be as formal or informal as you make it. Grab a meal with your big and get to know the club a bit more? Cool. Travel around the world with your newfound twin and share all ur clothes and deepest darkest secrets and internet search history? Das cool too. You do you. As long as you found a new buddy :)
