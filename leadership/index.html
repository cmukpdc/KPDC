Dance Instructors

About

Dance instructors teach our open classes and lead our subunits. To be considered a KPDC dance instructor, you must first be reviewed by the dance directors.

Interested in subunit leading?

Subunit leaders for each semester are chosen from the current KPDC dance instructors. If you are not a dance instructor and want to lead a subunit, please undergo the dance instructor review process.

Review Process

Prepare a segment of a dance you learned yourself to teach to the dance directors. Email the dance directors to schedule a time to audition.
