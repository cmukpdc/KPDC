Official Subunits

About

Official subunits are dances that anybody can join at the beginning of the semester and are performed at the KPDC Showcase. There is a matching process to ensure fair assignments and to prevent overflow.

New this Semester!

This semester there will be 2 types of official subunits - Open and Audition.

Open Subunits

Open subunits are first-come first-serve and are a great way to be a part of dance if it's your first time.

Audition Subunits

Audition subunits are more skills-based than open subunits so you will need to attend an audition for each audition subunit you are interested in.

Subunit matching is starting the 2nd week of school! The schedule for the subunit open classes will be available soon.

Subunit Matching Process

When/Where?

The 2nd week of school. Attend open classes for your favorite dances during the week. The times and places listed will be the subunit's normal practices throughout the semester.

How to Participate

Fill out the subunit ranking form (by Jan. 27th 11:59pm) which will be available January 21st. Make sure to fill out this form as soon as possible since open subunits are FIRST COME FIRST SERVE.

If you are interested in an OPEN SUBUNIT you will also need to attend ONE of the open classes that will be taught during the 2nd week of school. The schedule for the open class will be posted on this page soon!

If you are interested in an AUDITION SUBUNIT you will also need to attend the audition for each of the audition subunits you are interested in.

Results

After the 2nd week of school, we will review your rankings and progress in the open class to match you to a subunit!
