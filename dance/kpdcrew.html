KPDCrew

About

KPDCrew is K-Pop Dance Club's rotational dance crew for more advanced dancers looking for a more intensive group in KPDC. KPDCrew is dedicated to performing and filming dance covers of (usually) newer releases within a shorter time frame than subunits do. KPDCrew holds auditions every semester to welcome new members. Weekly time committment varies, but individual members are highly encouraged to practice individually in addition to scheduled weekly group practices.

Audition Process

Choose 1-2 K-Pop dances to perform segments of during the audition. Participants will be asked to perform their dances for the dance directors and other auditionees.
