Side Projects

About

While official subunits are formed through KPDC, projects allow members to form their own dance groups however they would like to, but are not guaranteed the same support subunits receive.

Join this Facebook group to view open, ongoing, and closed projects! All project creation, recruitment, and related news will occur in this group.

If you have any other questions regarding Projects contact Anne He on Facebook or email her at afhe@andrew.cmu.edu!

Project Rules

1. Anybody can claim any song as a project, including projects and subunits that have been done before and duplicates.

2. When making a post about a project, you need to include the following information:

  • Who is/are the project leader(s)?

  • Is the project open to all or by audition?

  • How many spots are still available?

  • Are you trying to perform for showcase, perform in general, or just for a video?

  • Who should be contacted, and how, if interested?

  • A link to the dance

3. All posts in this group will be reviewed before being posted.

4. Mark the post as [OPEN] while spots are still available. Once a dance is full, please edit posts as [CLOSED] to indicate that it is full.

5. Subunits get first priority for a song.

6. Projects must contact Lexin Yuan about filming at least 1 week before the date they plan to film.

7. Projects are not eligible for showcase if they have been performed at showcase within the past 4 years.

FAQ

Why exactly are you doing this?

Subunits are great because they allow you to mingle, grow, and dance with other dancers in a consistent time and place. Everyone is guaranteed a subunit spot as well as a guaranteed chance to perform in the KPDC showcase. Subunits are formed for the purpose of giving everyone an opportunity to dance. However, projects allow members who feel stifled by the subunit process to perform their desired dance, but are not guaranteed full club support or a spot in showcase.

What if I don't have group members for my project?

You have the choice of either handpicking your members or looking for interested members in the Facebook group.

Can I still sign up for subunits/other projects?

Yes! You are free to try for subunits and other projects.

What if multiple people reserve the same project at the same time?

The first come, first serve rule has been removed, so this is perfectly fine. Both projects can film, but only one can perform at showcase
